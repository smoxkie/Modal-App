WSF History

The Women’s Sports Foundation was established in 1974 to
advance the lives of women and girls through sports and physical
activity. Our mission is to enable all girls and women to reach their
potential in sports and life. We provide financial fuel to aspiring
champion athletes. We fund groundbreaking research.
We educate. We advocate. And we help communities get girls active.
Sure, there’s a long way to go but we’re not going to stop until we
get there.
